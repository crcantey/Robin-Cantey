How to make a Turkey Sandwhich

  1. Start by making sure you have the nessisary supplies in your home kitchen. Presliced container of Tirkey, Load of sliced bread, paper towel, sink with running water and soap
  2. wash your hands as you have done many times in your life
  3. Lay 1 paper towel sheet on your countertop
  4. Open your loaf of bread using one hands to hold the plastic bagged loaf and the other hand to remove bag closure clip
  5. lay down the plastic bag closure clip and let go of bag/loaf
  6. use one hand to open the bag to the loaf of bread
  7. Use your oppsosite hand to pull out 2 slices of bread
  8. Use both hands lay the bread slices side by side on the paper towel that is already on the counter
  9. Use both hand and historical reference to open the turkey
  10. Use one hand to pull out the desired amount of turkey slices
  11. Place the turkey slices on one slice of bread, let go of turkey
  12. Use one hand to pick up the slice of bread with no turkey and lay it on the the other turkey covered slice of bread
  13. Congratulations! You now have a very plain turkey sandwhich
